Exhibition

Dear Sydneysiders and other friends from around Australia and the world,


We would like to cordially invite you and your family to the 19th Annual Sculpture by the Sea. Sculpture by the Sea tranforms Bondi to Tamarama coastal walk into a spectacular, 2km long temporary sculpture park featuring over 100 sculptures by artists from Australia and across the world.

Event will be held from 22 October to 8 November 2015, located from Bondi to Tamarama coastal walk, Sydney, Australia. This event is free to public,and would be an exciting opportunity to learn and understand about visual art through sculpture form while enjoying the beautiful scenery along Bondi to Tamarama

We also suggest you to plan your trip to Bondi with the help of NSWTransport in advance to ensure you can get to the exhibition conveniently.

We hope to see you soon!


Peace, Love & Happiness,
