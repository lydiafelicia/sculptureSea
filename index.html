Sculpture by the Sea is a public arts events that tranforms Bondi to Tamarama coastal walk into a spectacular, 2km long temporary sculpture park featuring over 100 sculptures by artists from Australia and across the world.
This year's exhibition, which would be the 19th annual exhibition, will be held from 22 October to 8 November 2015, located from Bondi to Tamarama coastal walk, Sydney, Australia.
